Case Study: A Modern Web Presence for Carson, Real Estate Agent

Carson's Property Listings

A custom portfolio for managing pre-construction properties

The Problem

Carson, an ambitious agent, was limited by a generic brokerage profile page. He lacked a unique brand identity online, had no effective way to showcase his exclusive pre-construction projects, and was facing expensive, restrictive API fees for a standard MLS integration.

The Solution

  • A fully responsive, mobile-first design ensuring a perfect experience on any device.

  • A custom-built backend system that allows Carson to easily add, edit, and manage his pre-construction listings manually, giving him full control without expensive, recurring API fees.

  • Strategically placed calls-to-action to guide potential buyers and sellers toward booking a consultation.

  • A dedicated 'About' page to build personal trust and differentiate him from competitors.
